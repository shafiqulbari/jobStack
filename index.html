Frequently Asked Questions

A few quick answers to help you decide.

Where does the data come from?

We aggregate public listings from LinkedIn, Stack Overflow, and vetted job feeds via APIs and ethical scraping. Personally identifiable data is never stored.

How accurate are the trend charts?

Noise is reduced with de‑duplication, outlier removal, and rolling averages. Pro/Teams unlock faster refresh and deeper slices.

Is my profile private?

Yes. Your skills are stored securely and used only to compute your match score. You can delete them anytime.

Can I export charts?

Absolutely. PNG and CSV exports are available on Pro and Teams.
